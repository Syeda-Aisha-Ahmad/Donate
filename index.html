Our Donate Plan

Our aim is to ensure at least food, clothing, shelter and education for the children of vulnerable people.

Food for a family

The disaster has caused a food crisis. Let every human being satisfy their hunger and let no one get sick without eating.

Shelter for a family

Every school is being developed as a shelter. In addition, we are building new roofs to give shelter more families.

Education for a child

Why will the children's education stop in this disaster? We are collecting their textbooks so that they do not fall behind in the disaster.

Their lives depend on your donation

Disasters have taken away their own hard-working homes, they have lost their families, they have lost their possessions.
We should stand by them so that they can overcome this bad time. A little donation from each of us can make their present time a little easier. Donate with an open mind.
